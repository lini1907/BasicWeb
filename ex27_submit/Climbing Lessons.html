Climbing Lessons

At The Colorado Experience we provide several levels of climbing instruction, all tailored to your strengths, interests, and comfort levels. You can start on small one and two pitch climbs and move up to larger routes. Lessons are always under the watchful and encouraging eye of an experienced instructor.

Note: Students may be removed from courses for safety reasons at any time based on instructor discretion. Partial refunds are available for uncompleted courses.

Introduction to Rock Climbing I

Time: Half day | Prerequisites: None | Rating: 5.5 - 5.6

Introduction to Rock Climbing II

Time: 1 day | Prerequisites: Basic experience or Intro I | Rating: 5.6 - 5.7

Intermediate to Advanced Climbing

Time: 2 days | Prerequisites: Instructor evaluation | Rating: 5.8 - 5.9

Aid Climbing

Time: 2 days | Prerequisites: Instructor evaluation | Rating: A1 - A3

Ice Climbing

Time: 1 day | Prerequisites: Instructor evaluation and seasonal conditions.
