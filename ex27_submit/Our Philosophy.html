Our Philosophy

The Colorado Experience is more than a climbing school; it is a business dedicated to teaching the sport and art of climbing while building relationships that allow our students and instructors to reach their highest potential. It is a place for you to fulfill your dreams, to do things you thought you could not do, and to achieve a deeper appreciation of our wonderful outdoors.

At The Colorado Experience, we strive to ensure that all our students grow in the art of climbing and in the love of the outdoors through:

  • Individual attention
  • Customized climbs tailored to each student's strengths and interests
  • Uncompromised safety
  • Professional guides and support staff
  • Emphasis on environment awareness and protection

Here, you are more than students, you are partners in The Colorado Experience.
